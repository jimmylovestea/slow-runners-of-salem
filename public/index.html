About Us

Running is hard. But it’s also a lot more fun and enjoyable when you run with friends. So we, the Slow Runners of Salem, follow two beliefs:

All faces and all paces welcome
No one runs alone

Our runs are typically 2-4 miles long (Walking breaks welcome!) and our runners run anywhere from sub 10-minute miles to slower than 12-minute miles.

Whether you’re new to running or are training to set new PRs, we hope to see you sometime to jog a few miles with us.
